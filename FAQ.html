Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Zodiac Sign?

The position of the planets on the day you were born make up your Zodiac Sign.

What is Astrology?

Astrology is the observation of how the sun, moon, and planets interact, and how those interactions affect us here on Earth.(Corrine, 2020)

Does studying Astrology make you an Atheist?

Astrology studies the effects of celestial bodies on earthly events, such as the moon's impact on ocean tides. Although often considered a belief system, astrology is based on scientific observation rather than a belief (or lack thereof) in the Creator.
